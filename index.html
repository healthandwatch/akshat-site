Welcome to My Website

Research Student, University of California Irvine

My primary area of expertise is mathematics, including analysis, algebra, and probability theory. I am particularly interested in dynamical systems, hyperbolic and partially hyperbolic dynamics, and their connection to other areas of mathematics. Most beautiful results in mathematics usually appear on the edge of different areas, and I am most interested in questions that involve dynamical systems and other areas of mathematics, such as spectral theory, mathematical physics, random matrix products, celestial mechanics, and even mathematical logic. Here you can learn more about my research.

I completed my undergraduate studies at UC Irvine, where I developed a strong foundation in pure mathematics. Through my research, I continue to explore the fundamental structures underlying complex mathematical systems.
